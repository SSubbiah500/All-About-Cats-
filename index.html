Overview

The cat, or house cat is the only domesticated species of the family Felidae. Cats are carnivorous creatures that either hunt or scavenge for their food and their average lifespan is about 12-18 years. There are about 40-70 different breeds of cats. Certain breeds will not only have different physical features like fur color, fur texture, or ear shape, but they will also have different behaviors.
