Most Beautiful Cat Breeds

There are over 40-70 different cat breeds but the ones on this list are the most unique and beautiful.

Name Picture Physical Characteristics Behavior
Munchkin Cat Munchkins have short legs from a natural genetic mutation. They have short or long coats in any color. Munchkins are very active and they enjoy climbing on things. They are very friendly and sociable.
Siamese Cat Siamese are slender cats with short coats. They have darkened fur around the face, ears, legs, and tail plus blue eyes. Siamese cats are socail and friendly. They are extremely loyal cats that form extremely strong bonds with certain people.
Sphynx Cat Sphynx cats are hairless with warm, wrinkly skin and large ears. They have large bellies. Sphynx cats are social and require a lot of attention. They love cuddling under blankets with humans.
Bombay Cat Bombay cats have short black fur and long legs. They can have green or copper-golden eyes. They are very affectionate and often rub against their owners. Bombay cats love playing games and learning tricks.
Oriental Shorthair Cat Oriental Shorthairs are slender cats with short fur. Their fur can be in any color and their eyes are usually blue or green. They constantly want attention from humans and they are very playful and tend to vocalize a lot.
Ragdoll Ragdolls usually have white fur around most of their body with darker fur around their ears or tail. They have blue eyes. Ragdolls can be aggressive at times, but overall they are very affectionate cats. They are known for sitting on your keyboard or even demanding to be held for long periods of time.
Korat Korats have gray, almost silver-looking fur with large ears and green eyes. Korats are extremely curious about their surroundings and they are sociable cats. They love to cuddle and play with humans.
American Wirehair Wirehairs have a thick coat that can be white or brown in color. They can have blue, green, or gold eyes. Wirehairs are easygoing cats that love to play, but don't constantly demand it. Wirehairs form strong bonds with humans.
Manx They have wide ears, a short back, and a very small tail (almost no tail at all). They have longer hind legs. They are very active and playful and are known for jumping a lot.
